this charming rocketsled of a vehicle is a 1982 diesel Chevette, which has been converted to run on waste vegetable oil (WVO). WVO flows freely like the nectar of the gods from the deep fryers of America. I installed the Greasel kit, and I'm convinced that the Isuzu / Chevy engineers were sitting around drinking cheap American beers when they decided to covertly design a perfect engine bay for grease conversions. America. Now I've mentioned it thrice. I love my homeland, but please America, don't bomb anyone for petroleum anymore. make food not bombs. Support alternative transportation methods, such as cycling, bussing, biofuels, monorailing (yah seattle!), footing, and not driving a SUV!
