title: Portrait of a WVO Vehicle, in Three Movements
artist: anonymous
materials: haiku, vim 

Movement I: prelude mistico

the shaking of leaf
the life of morning returns
a diesel Chevette


Movement II: l'apres-midi d'un Diesel Chevette

grease burns, pistons push
spinning wheels, joyful pilots
the carbon cycle


Movement III: rest

injector meets derv
liberating CO2
cleansing.  renewal.
